Welcome to CourseChat!

Empowering your study sessions with advanced LLM technology.

At CourseChat, we harness the power of LlamaIndex to change how students interact with lecture materials. Here's how to make the most of it:

  • Browse Available Courses: Start by exploring the courses we offer. Each course is equipped with a specialized chatbot, ready to assist you.
  • Ask Specific Questions: Got a question? Just ask! Our chatbots can provide detailed explanations, clarify doubts, and guide you through complex topics.
  • Engage with Lecture Materials: Use our platform to delve deeper into your study materials. It's like having a personal tutor at your fingertips.

By integrating LlamaIndex's LLM capabilities, CourseChat aims to create an inclusive, supportive, and effective learning environment for everyone. Let's make learning an adventure!
